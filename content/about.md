Hey, I'm **Mostafa** — a computer engineer who roams across many domains, always chasing the *why* behind how things work. When I'm not in code, I'm into music and BodyPump classes.

I'm particularly drawn to fundamentals: how systems are built, how abstractions leak, and what sits underneath what we take for granted.

My first real language was Objective-C (yes, before Swift existed — and yes, HTML doesn't count). From there I wandered into Computer Vision, then Embedded programming, and now I find myself deep in compilers, Rust, LLMs, and hermetic build systems. The interests shift; the curiosity doesn't.
